THE PROJECT

## NOT A GENRE.  A LANGUAGE.

MISE is a live, soulful and experimental music project rooted in self-expression and cultural exploration. Led by Seán, MISE draws from neo-soul, jazz and hip-hop, blending rich harmonies with Dilla-inspired rhythms, abstract rap, and Irish language and cultural influences.

The name MISE, meaning "myself" in Irish, speaks to both identity and intention. It reflects an unpretentious, genre-fluid approach where music is treated as a direct extension of the self rather than a fixed category.

Live instrumentation at the core

Multiple sold-out shows

Worked with artists including Sonnyjim
